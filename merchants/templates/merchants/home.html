About KYB Compliance

Know Your Business (KYB) is a critical compliance process that helps payment providers verify the legitimacy of merchant businesses before allowing them to process payments. Our system performs:

  • Risk Assessment - Evaluate business category and jurisdiction risk
  • Beneficial Ownership - Identify ultimate beneficial owners
  • Sanctions Screening - Check against OFAC, UN, and other sanctions lists
  • PEP Screening - Identify politically exposed persons
